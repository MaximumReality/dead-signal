# 📟 DEAD SIGNAL — v1.1

> **"The signal is fading. The grid is compromised. Stay alive."**

**Dead Signal** is a fast-paced, neon-drenched cyberpunk survival shooter built by [Maximum Reality](https://maximumreality.xyz). Fight off waves of glitch-zombies, manage your ammo, and utilize experimental signal tech to survive the collapse.

Created as a special project for **Seth**.

---

## 🕹️ Gameplay Mechanics

You are a survivor trapped in a collapsing digital grid. As waves of zombies (or "pixel-shifters") breach the perimeter, you must eliminate them to keep the signal stable.

### 🛠 The Arsenal
* **Neon Pistol:** Your primary defense. 30 rounds per magazine.
* **Vitals Monitor:** Real-time health tracking. If the signal drops to 0%, connection is terminated.
* **Glitch Feedback:** As your vitals drop, the reality around you will begin to distort and hue-shift.

### 💎 Power-Ups (The Azul & Mochkil Protocol)
Special drops may appear upon neutralizing hostiles:
* **⬡ SIGNAL BUFFER (Azul’s Tech):** Overclocks your perception. Reality slows down (30% speed) while you maintain full movement, allowing for precise tactical shots.
* **🍰 SNACK ATTACK (Mochkil’s Special):** A chaotic surge of energy. Grants **Infinite Ammo** and **Double Movement Speed** for a short duration.
