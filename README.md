# 📟 DEAD SIGNAL — Maximum Reality

> **"The signal is fading. The grid is compromised. Stay alive."**

**Dead Signal** is a fast-paced, neon-drenched cyberpunk survival shooter. Fight off waves of glitch-zombies, manage your ammo, and utilize experimental signal tech to survive the collapse.

---

## 🕹️ Game Versions

### 🔵 [Main Branch (Standard)](https://maximumreality.github.io/dead-signal/)
The core survival experience. Pure skill, limited ammo, and increasing wave intensity.
* **Focus:** Resource management and tactical movement.

### 🟣 [Drops Edition (Experimental)](https://maximumreality.github.io/dead-signal/drops.html)
The "Maximum Reality" overclocked version featuring the **Azul & Mochkil Protocol**.
* **⬡ SIGNAL BUFFER:** Slows time to 30% speed for precision hits.
* **🍰 SNACK ATTACK:** Grants Infinite Ammo and Double Speed.
* **Visual Glitch Engine:** Reality distorts as your vitals drop.
